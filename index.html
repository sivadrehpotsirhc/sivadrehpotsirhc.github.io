About this shop

The Foundry is one bench, one hammer, and a pile of ideas that occasionally cool into usable shapes. No accounts, no installers phoning home, no newsletter popup asking for your firstborn. You download the tool, the tool works. That's the whole business model (there is no business model).

Found a crack in the casting? Something rattles when you shake it? Bring it back to the bench and complain (it's the only way i'll notice probably).
